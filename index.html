About Holly & Bloom

We’re a small, locally owned grooming studio serving Eden, NC and surrounding communities. Our focus is on low-stress, one-on-one grooming so your pet never feels rushed or overwhelmed.

We schedule fewer pets per day to keep the environment calm and quiet. That means more time for gentle handling, breaks when needed, and a grooming experience tailored to your pet’s age, health, and personality.
